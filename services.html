Telomere Testing

  • We all know that your chronological age is the number of years you have been alive since your birth. But do you know your biological age? It might not be the same.​ A telomere test is a blood test that measures the length of telomeres. Telomeres are the protective structures located at the tips of your chromosomes. Telomere testing will show us your estimated biological age and is measured by the changes in the physical structures of the body (such as organs and bones), sensory perception, and motor skills. Knowing your biological age can give you insights into your health, lifespan and susceptibility to diseases.
